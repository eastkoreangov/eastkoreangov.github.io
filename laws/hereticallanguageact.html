The Heretical Language Act

This law criminalises the following ideas, lifestyles and beliefs:

  • Capitalism
  • Round Earthism
  • The belief that birds are real animals not government surveillance drones
  • Furries and furryism

The following people are exempt from this law:

  • Those who follow those views due to a lack of knowledge
  • Those who express those views satirically to ridicule them

Those found to be breaking the law shall be sent to the gulag
