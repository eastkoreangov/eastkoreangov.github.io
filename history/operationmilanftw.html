Operation #MilanForTheWin

Overview

Operation #MilanForTheWin (Latin East Korean: Operäcio #MilanEstasLaVenko) was the code name for an operation carried out by Milánists that occured in the Sports Hall of St Bede's Inter-Church School, a secondary school in Cambridge, Cambridgeshire in the Province of The United Republic, East Korea. Its aim was to make Milán Erdős, also known as Comrade Milenin, be the sole winner of a game of dodgeball in a PE lesson.

Successes and Failures

The Milánists, who all suffered at least one causalty each, succeeded in getting Milán into the top 6 but not in making him the sole winner of the dodgeball game. An unknow anti-Milánist at one point killed Milán, meaning he suffered one causalty. However, no one won the game so it was debated whether the operation was successful or not. In the end a vote found that most people think it was successful.

Aftermath

After the battle the Milánist cult The Cause was created, which believes that all of the high powers answer to Milán. Within the first 5 seconds of its creation it gained 10 members, making it the fastest growing religion at that time.
