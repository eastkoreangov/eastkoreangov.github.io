The Food Act

This law defines heretical food as the following:
  • Mushrooms, excluding mushroom cream soup where mushrooms are present as a broth only
This law bans the purchase, consumption and export of heretical foods People who have a heretical food permit are exempted from this ban on purchasing and consuming but not exporting.
Local authorities may hand out heretical food permits to the following people:
  • Scientists and researches using mushrooms for scientific research
  • Food reviewers who give the heretical food a bad rating
